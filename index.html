About us

A calm, premium learning space managed by JEF Charitable Trust.

The Imphal Valley School is managed by JEF Charitable TRUST, driven by the desire to offer the best possible education for pupils.

The campus is located near the city, yet away from city noise and pollution, with large secluded grounds and open spaces for co-curricular activities and environmental studies.
